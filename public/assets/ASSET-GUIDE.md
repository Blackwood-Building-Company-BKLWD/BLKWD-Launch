# BLKWD Asset Guide

Drop your high-end renders and media into the following folders:

## `/assets/renders/`
- `hero-poster.jpg` — Hero section poster image (16:9, min 1920px wide)
- `luxury-home-01.jpg` — Luxury Custom Homes block (4:3 ratio)
- `knockdown-rebuild-01.jpg` — KDR block (4:3 ratio)
- `signature-01.jpg` through `signature-04.jpg` — Gallery grid (3:2 ratio)
- `ig-01.jpg` through `ig-09.jpg` — Instagram grid posts (1:1 square)

## `/assets/video/`
- `hero-reel.mp4` — Hero background video (16:9, H.264, max 15MB)

## `/assets/textures/`
- Optional background textures and overlays

## `/fonts/`
- `CreatoDisplay-ExtraBold.woff2` / `.woff`
- `CreatoDisplay-Bold.woff2` / `.woff`
- `CreatoDisplay-Medium.woff2` / `.woff`

All images should be optimized (WebP/AVIF preferred) and sized for retina displays.
